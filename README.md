# Investments in 1 Minute

AI-powered Telegram Robo-Advisor for CIS investors.

Investments in 1 Minute helps users analyze stocks, ETFs, portfolios, and financial goals in under one minute using market data, portfolio intelligence, Shariah-compliance screening, and automated investment planning.

Built specifically for Russian-speaking investors who face high inflation, limited financial education, and a lack of accessible ethical investing tools.

---

## The Problem

Many people across the CIS struggle to invest effectively because:

- inflation steadily erodes cash savings
- financial information is fragmented and difficult to interpret
- investment advice on social media is often emotional or misleading
- portfolio management tools are designed for experienced investors
- Shariah-compliant investing support is extremely limited

As a result, many individuals either avoid investing entirely or make decisions without a structured framework.

---

## The Solution

Investments in 1 Minute combines:

- Investment Analytics
- Portfolio Intelligence
- Goal Planning
- Automated Investment Allocation
- Shariah Compliance Screening

inside a single Telegram interface.

The product transforms complex financial analysis into actionable investment decisions.

---

## Core Features

### Stock & ETF Analysis

Analyze any stock or ETF using:

- financial fundamentals
- historical growth
- risk metrics
- AI-generated key takeaways
- Shariah-compliance screening

Output is optimized for beginner and intermediate investors.

---

### Portfolio Intelligence

Users can create and manage a virtual portfolio.

Portfolio analysis includes:

- portfolio value
- diversification assessment
- concentration risk
- portfolio volatility
- risk scoring
- compliance exposure

---

### Financial Goals

Users can create long-term financial goals.

The system calculates:

- probability of success
- progress milestones
- goal scores
- personalized recommendations

using portfolio and contribution data.

---

### Auto-Invest Engine

The Auto-Invest system converts:

monthly budget → target allocation → investment plan

Instead of showing percentages,
the system generates actionable investment decisions.

---

### Shariah Compliance System

The platform includes a proprietary Shariah-screening layer for:

- individual stocks
- ETFs
- entire portfolios

Features include:

- business activity screening
- financial ratio screening
- portfolio-level compliance analysis
- compliance exposure tracking

---

### AI Portfolio Insights

The Financial Brain generates:

- portfolio diagnostics
- strategic recommendations
- risk observations
- goal-based insights

designed to explain not only what to do,
but why.
